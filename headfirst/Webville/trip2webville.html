    The steps to register under your own domain name is too...
    1) Find a hosting company like Dreamhost or Bluehost and tel them about your want. Often they will tell you that you can get one in one of their deals
    2) Come up with your domain name, pick a suffix.
    3) Purchase the domain name, add domain ID Protection to protect your domain
    4) Some places to regesiter are Google, Catch Domains, and Claimeddomains
